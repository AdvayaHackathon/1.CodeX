Emergency Preparation Tips

Medical Preparation

  • Carry essential medications
  • Have a basic first aid kit
  • Keep medical insurance card
  • Know blood type and allergies
  • Research medical facilities at destination

Document Safety

  • Photograph all travel documents
  • Email docs to yourself & trusted person
  • Carry photocopies separate from originals
  • Register with embassy if available
  • Keep emergency contacts accessible

Communication Plan

  • Download offline maps of your area
  • Save emergency phrases in local language
  • Have backup battery/power bank
  • Set check-in schedule with family
  • Know how to call internationally
